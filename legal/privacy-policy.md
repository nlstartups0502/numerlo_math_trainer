# Privacy Policy for Numerlo - Math Trainer

**Last Updated: January 17, 2026**

## Introduction

Numerlo - Math Trainer ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to your use of our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

Numerlo - Math Trainer is designed to work entirely on your device without collecting any user data. Specifically:

- We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
- We do not track your location
- We do not collect game scores or progress data
- We do not use analytics or tracking tools
- We do not collect device identifiers or usage statistics

## How the App Works

Numerlo - Math Trainer is a math quiz game that operates entirely on your device:

- Math questions are generated locally on your device
- Your scores and timer results are stored only on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No user accounts or authentication are required

## Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, including:

- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting tools
- Cloud storage services

## Children's Privacy

Numerlo - Math Trainer does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect any data, there is no user data to secure or protect on our servers. All game progress remains on your device.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
