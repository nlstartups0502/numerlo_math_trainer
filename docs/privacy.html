Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Introduction

Numerlo - Math Trainer ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to your use of our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Numerlo - Math Trainer collects minimal information to provide our services:

Information You Provide (Optional)

Email Address:

  • You may optionally provide an email address to participate in the leaderboard feature
  • This is entirely optional - you can use all core features without providing an email
  • If provided, we use your email to identify you on the leaderboard and for account-related communications
  • We do not share your email with third parties

Information We Automatically Collect

Leaderboard Data:

  • If you choose to participate in the leaderboard, we collect your game scores and points
  • This data is displayed publicly on the leaderboard (associated with a username or email if provided)
  • You can opt out of the leaderboard at any time

Local Data:

  • Your game progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device
  • This local data is not transmitted to our servers unless you opt into the leaderboard feature

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data

Third-Party Services

Apple In-App Purchases:

  • Subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store
  • Apple handles all payment processing and billing information
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles your data

We do not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Other third-party tracking services

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

Email Address (if provided):

  • To identify you on the leaderboard
  • To send important updates about the app (you can opt out)
  • To provide customer support if you contact us

Leaderboard Data:

  • To display rankings and scores to other users
  • To create a competitive gaming experience

We will never:

  • Sell your information to third parties
  • Use your email for marketing without your consent
  • Share your data except as described in this policy

Data Storage and Security

Server Storage:

  • If you opt into the leaderboard, your email (if provided) and scores are stored on our secure servers
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS

Local Storage:

  • Game progress and settings that are not part of the leaderboard remain on your device
  • You can delete local data by uninstalling the app

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Opt out of leaderboard: You can stop participating in the leaderboard at any time through the app settings
  • Delete your data: Contact us to request deletion of your email and leaderboard data
  • Access your data: Request a copy of the data we have about you
  • Use without email: You can use all core features without providing an email address

Subscription Information

Subscription Terms:

  • Numerlo offers monthly and annual subscription plans
  • All subscriptions include a 3-day free trial
  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled
  • You can cancel anytime through your Apple ID settings

Billing:

  • All billing is handled by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • Refunds are subject to Apple's refund policy

Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy seriously. While Numerlo - Math Trainer is appropriate for users of all ages:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • The leaderboard and email features are optional and not required to use the app
  • Parents can contact us to request deletion of their child's data at any time
  • We recommend parental supervision for children using the leaderboard feature

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13 without parental consent, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the app
  • Leaderboard data: Retained while you participate in the leaderboard
  • Email addresses: Retained until you request deletion or delete your account
  • Inactive accounts: We may delete data from accounts inactive for more than 2 years

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
